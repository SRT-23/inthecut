About The shop

Opened in 2019 In The Cut Barbershop has quickly become the premier barbershop in all of Salt Lake county. With 6 barbers to choose from, we provide unique styles and great customer service at an affordable price. We are also the only barbershop in Utah with a sneaker wall where customers are able to look at and purchase awesome sneakers. We strive to do everything to make your experience a great one whether you're a new customer of an existing one. Book an appointment or walk-in and get IN the cut.
